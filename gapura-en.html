Frequently asked

Gapura is an Indonesian technology-based financial service solution focusing on Payment Gateway service, actively connecting with banks, e-wallets, and other elements in the digital economy ecosystem to serve SMEs to large enterprises with our customizable integration solutions.

Setting up is free, and we only charge a fee for every successful transaction. There is no implementation or maintenance fee. For detailed pricing of all payment methods, please See Pricing.

Register your account here and follow further instructions.

Gapura is suitable for businesses of all sizes, from small businesses to large enterprises, including:

  • E-commerce businesses: Gapura offers a variety of payment methods that are well-suited for e-commerce businesses, including credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets, and buy now, pay later (BNPL) options.
  • Retail businesses: We offer an array of in-store payment solutions tailored to the needs of retail businesses. Our comprehensive range includes cutting-edge point-of-sale (POS) systems, convenient mobile payments, and secure contactless payment options..
  • SaaS businesses: We provide a variety of payment methods that are ideal for SaaS businesses, including recurring payments, subscription payments, and in-app payments.
  • Platforms and marketplaces: Discover a range of payment methods tailored for platforms and marketplaces, including in-app payments, cross-border transactions, and multi-currency support.
